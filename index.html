Nomination Guidelines

How to Nominate

Read all guidelines carefully before submitting. Nominations are assessed by the GEAA judging committee based on evidence and criteria.

👤

Who Can Nominate

  • Alumni may submit a Self-Nomination.
  • Alumni may nominate a Fellow Alumnus/a.
  • Staff & faculty members may also nominate alumni.
  • A nominator may propose multiple alumni across different categories.
📋

Submission Requirements

  • Review all award categories before nominating.
  • Provide relevant proof(s) supporting the nomination.
  • Clearly describe the nominee's qualities, contributions, and worthiness.
  • All mandatory fields in the Nomination Form must be completed.
⚖️

Judging Process

  • Nominations are judged on award criteria and evidence provided.
  • The GEAA judging committee reviews all eligible applications.
  • Winners will be honoured at the Bangalore Meetup 2027.
📅

Key Deadline

Feb 15, 2027

All nominations must be submitted before this date. Late submissions will not be considered.

Judging Criteria

Four Pillars of Evaluation

01

Impact

Evidence of tangible, measurable impact — and its scale — in your profession, community, or society, inspired by your GEU education.

02

GU Influence

Evidence of how your Graphic Era education played a key role in shaping your success and amplifying your impact.

03

Media Traction

The degree to which your success story is likely to resonate with local, national, or global media in your country of entry or beyond.

04

Inspire Potential

Your capacity and willingness to influence the next generation of students to choose Graphic Era as their study destination.
